Bob is a homeless man with a beautiful soul. He greets people everyday as they walk pass his fortress made of cupboard boxes and scrap metals along the roadside, place he called home for so many years now. One day, Bob stubbled over an envelope covered with dirt. Inside it he found a bundle of cash worth $1,000,000.
"Today is my lucky day. I've never had this much money in my entire life, and now I'm holding it in my hand", he said with tears of joy running down his eyes.
